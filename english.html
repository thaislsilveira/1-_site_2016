About me

Learn a little about the person behind this blog.

My name is Thaís Laine Neves da Silveira, I'm 19 years old, I live in Jales, I have technical training in administrative management at SENAI in Votuporanga and I'm currently studying Systems for Internet at Fatec Jales.

I am passionate about animals, games and anime, my first contact with computers was at 13 years old, in a lan house.

When I had my first computer my curiosities about that machine increased, but I did not think I could understand it.

As time passed, I dared to try to understand it, with research and my entry into Fatec Jales, my focus today is to finish the course and maybe to encourage new girls to join and love this area as I do.
